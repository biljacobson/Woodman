Woodman Logging, LLC

Family business providing tree and wood services to the Metro Boston and Worcester areas.
Licensed and fully insured.

Services we offer

In addition to the services listed below, we can work with you on any special requests you may have.
The job isn't complete until you are satisfied.

Mobile Woodsplitting

We can provide wood splitting at your site or property. We can cut and split your logs or wood rounds to your desired size.

Tree removal & trimming

We provde tree removal and/or trimming services. Whether the tree is healthy, dead, or damaged from a storm, we can provide removal from your property.

Firewood sales

We sell seasoned and green hardwoods. Most hardwoods are oak, cherry, maple, or ash. Free delivery.

Pricing

Pricing will vary job to job, to receive an accurate estimate we will need to see the tree/wood in person.
The size of the tree/wood and how accessible it is with a truck both factor into the final price.
